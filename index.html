About Us

Chosen generation is a youth charity group with the aim to reach the children that are homeless. The group was initially started in the year 2016 by two groups of students thats is 3 East Githiga Boys high school whose leader was Joseph Ngugi and 2 West Rarakwa Girls high school whose leader was Victorial Wangari. The two classes unintentionally first met at an event in ruiru Watu wa Maana Centre on 5th december 2016. They both agree to combine their teams and work together as one group and on 16th december 2016 the member held a meeting at Kasarani and the groups merged officially under the name Chosen Generation which was under the reference of 1st peter 2:9 "We are the chosen generation".

The group has continued to grow as the member invite friends to come together and help the helpless and also giving hope to the hopeless. We have been to many childern's home where we make contributions were every member is expected to contribute ksh 600 which are then used to purchase the basic item. The officials plan for an event the do some pre-visit to the childer's home, ask for their permission and set a date then the information is the communicated to the group members through a WhatsApp group. At the actual event we interact with the kid, play together, cook, wash their clothes and also have a session were we introduce ourselves and most importantly share the meal together.
